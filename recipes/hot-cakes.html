Hot cakes

Description

This recipe will help you to make delicious hot cakes in your home.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup dry farina cereal (such as Cream of Wheat®)
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
